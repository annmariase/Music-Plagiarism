ABOUT

A simple and objective analysis of music, with the aim of detecting similar or identical themes and motives, has been difficult so far, as appropriate devices and methods were missing. DupeScan is a website for music plagiarism detection which offers a number of tools that support the objective assessment of alleged music plagiarism.
